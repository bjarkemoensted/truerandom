Random Quantum Stuff

You probably have that annoying friend that insists that tossing a coin or having a computer generate a random choice isn't "random enough". Fortunately, some nice physicists at the Australian National University have set up this web service providing truly random numbers generated by quantum physics. I then made this web app to harness this quantum randomness to generate virtual coin tosses, dice throws, etc, that you can use to tell your friend to stfu.
